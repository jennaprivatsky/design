Hi, I'm Jenna!

My name is Jenna Privatsky, I am currently enrolled in the Design Program at the University of Minnesota — Twin Cities where I am pursuing a degree in Graphic Design coupled with a Product Design minor. I am preparing to graduate in May 2018, and look forward to see what opportunities await me.

I have been interested in art and design ever since I was young and have loved pursuing it professionally. Problem solving is a strength of mine and being able to solve design problems with aesthetic solutions is one of the things that I like most about design. Pairing my critical thinking with my creative problem solving is something that has brought me mounting success with both graphic design and product design projects. I enjoy all areas of design from print and layout design, to web design and development and everything in between. I welcome whatever design challenge comes my way!
